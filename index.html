More about me

Hello, I'm John. Gardening is my life passion and vocation. From an early age, I loved digging with my grandmother and grandfather in the ground and it has remained so until today.

In improving my passion, I provide gardening services with a wide spectrum. From the design of irrigation systems, their implementation and advancement in this field, to simple gardening work and professional lawn care. In addition, my specialty are exotic plants, I can take care of them like hardly anyone. I graduated from Berkley University in Garden Design and Irrigation Systems. Additionally, I completed courses in the knowledge of exotic, steppe and mountain plants. I am a speaker at the annual conventions on exotic plants.

If you want to work with me or you have exotic plants that need a special care, please contact me.
